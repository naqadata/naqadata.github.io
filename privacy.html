Naqafin Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Naqafin is a personal beta Roku client used to connect to a Jellyfin media server.

Naqafin does not sell personal information, display ads, or use third-party analytics or tracking services.

The app may store and use information needed to connect to the Jellyfin server you configure, such as server address, user account information, authentication tokens, playback state, and media library data returned by that server.

Your Jellyfin server controls the media library, account data, metadata, playback history, and related information available through the app.

Roku may collect device, platform, and usage information under Roku's own privacy policies.

Naqafin is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Jellyfin or Roku.
